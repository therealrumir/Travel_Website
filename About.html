About Agra

Location

Agra is a city located in Uttar Pradesh (UP),India. It hosts one of the most famous monuments in the world, The Taj Mahal.This city is situated on the banks of River Yamuna. It is about 230 kilometers away from Delhi the capital. It was founded by Sikandar II.

Famous Monuments

Agra is famous for several monuments. Some of these are:

  • Taj Mahal
  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehput Sikri
  • Akbar's Tomb

Food

Agra is known for its Mughlai flavors. The different cuisine and foods Agra is known are:

  • Petha
  • Bedai
  • Kachori
  • Chaat
  • Biryani
  • Jalebi

Uniqueness

Agra is a very unique city in India. It is known for its rich history, architectural marvels, and vibrant culture. It is known for:

  • The Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World
  • Mughal Architecture
  • Rich Cultural Heritage
  • Handicrafts and Marble Inlay Work
